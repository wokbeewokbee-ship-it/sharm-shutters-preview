Sharm Shutters

Tailored Shutters and Shading for Your Villa

Improve privacy, shade, outdoor comfort and access with made-to-measure solutions planned around your villa’s architecture and daily use.

One property, several needs

A villa may require different solutions across windows, doors, balconies, terraces and outdoor spaces. The team surveys the property and helps coordinate a consistent finish and suitable operation for each area.

Villa applications

  • Bedroom and living-area openings
  • Patio and balcony doors
  • Large windows
  • Entrances and service areas
  • Terraces and outdoor seating
  • Awnings and shading for sun-exposed areas

Benefits

  • Privacy
  • Shade
  • Outdoor comfort
  • Access
  • Coordinated appearance
  • On-site measurement

Manual or Electric Operation

Ask the team to recommend an operating method based on opening size, frequency of use, access and available electrical preparation.

Control options

  • Wall switch
  • Receiver and remote control
  • Smart Wi-Fi switch with mobile application

Warranty summary

  • 5 years on motors against manufacturing defects
  • 2 years on shutters and aluminium profiles against manufacturing defects
