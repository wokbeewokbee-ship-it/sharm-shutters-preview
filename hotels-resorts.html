Sharm Shutters

Shutter and Shading Solutions for Hospitality Properties

Support guest comfort, operational needs and the appearance of your property with solutions planned around hospitality environments.

Coordinated for hospitality operations

Hotel and resort projects may involve different areas, schedules and stakeholders. Sharm Shutters assesses each opening, recommends an appropriate solution and coordinates installation around the agreed site requirements.

Potential applications

  • Guest rooms and residential-style units
  • Balconies and terraces
  • Retail and service outlets within the property
  • Storage and operational areas
  • Entrances and commercial openings

For project and operations teams

Share drawings, opening schedules or site photos where available. A physical inspection is used to confirm measurements, access, power and finishing requirements.

  • Initial brief
  • Drawings or site photos
  • Physical inspection
  • Measurements and access review
  • Recommendation and quotation
  • Agreed installation coordination

Controls, Warranty & Support

Suitable controls

Depending on the selected motor and electrical preparation, suitable options may include a wall switch, receiver and remote control, or smart Wi-Fi switch with a compatible mobile application.

Warranty summary

  • 5 years on motors against manufacturing defects
  • 2 years on shutters and aluminium profiles against manufacturing defects

Maintenance support

Maintenance service, technical support and spare parts are available when required.
