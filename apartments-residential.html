Sharm Shutters

More Comfort and Privacy at Home

Tailored shutter and shading solutions help residents manage strong sunlight, privacy and daily comfort without compromising the appearance of the property.

Designed for everyday living

Whether the property is a permanent home, holiday apartment or rental unit, the solution should be easy to use and suited to the opening. Sharm Shutters measures on site and helps you choose a practical configuration.

Common goals

  • Reduce direct sunlight and glare
  • Increase privacy
  • Improve comfort in bedrooms and living areas
  • Add protection to windows, balconies or doors
  • Choose convenient motorized operation where suitable
  • Replace or service an existing electric shutter

Suitable areas

  • Bedrooms
  • Living rooms
  • Balconies
  • Kitchens
  • Entrances
  • Rental units

Operation, Controls & Warranty

Operation and control

  • Manual operation
  • Electric motor operation
  • Wall switch
  • Receiver switch and remote control
  • Smart Wi-Fi switch with mobile application

Warranty summary

  • 5 years on motors against manufacturing defects
  • 2 years on shutters and aluminium profiles against manufacturing defects
