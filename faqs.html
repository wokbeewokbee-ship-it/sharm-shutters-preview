Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about measurements, installation, electric shutters, maintenance and arranging a quotation in Sharm El Sheikh.

Questions and answers

Is the inspection free?

Yes. Inspection and measurements are free inside Sharm El Sheikh.

Which types of properties do you work with?

Solutions are available for apartments and homes, villas, commercial stores, restaurants and cafés, hotels and resorts, administrative offices, garages and warehouses, plus screens and display applications assessed on site.

What products do you offer?

The verified range includes roller shutters, awnings and shading solutions. Available materials, finishes, motors and configurations are confirmed during consultation.

Do I need exact measurements before contacting you?

No. Photos and approximate dimensions help with the first discussion, but the team confirms final measurements before production or installation.

How long does implementation take?

Timing depends on the measurements, quantity, selected system and site requirements. The expected schedule is confirmed with the detailed quotation.

Are manual and electric operation available?

Yes. Manual and electric configurations are available depending on the opening and project requirements.

Which control options are available?

Available control options include a wall switch, a receiver-enabled switch with remote control and a smart Wi-Fi switch operated through a compatible mobile application. Compatibility depends on the selected motor and electrical preparation.

Are several colours available?

Yes. Several colours are available depending on the profile and current availability. The supplied BK Shutter datasheet shows White, White Beige and Dark Gray.

Is there a warranty?

Yes. Sharm Shutters provides a 5-year warranty on motors and a 2-year warranty on shutters and aluminium profiles against manufacturing defects, subject to the approved project warranty terms.

Do you offer maintenance?

Yes. Maintenance, technical support and spare parts are available when required.

Which areas do you cover?

The verified service area is Sharm El Sheikh. Contact the team to confirm availability outside the city.

Can I visit the showroom?

Yes. The publicly listed showroom is at Jasmine Mall, Nabq Bay, Sharm El Sheikh. Confirm opening hours before visiting.

What should I send on WhatsApp?

Send your name, location, property type, photos or a short video, approximate dimensions if available and what you want the solution to achieve.

Still need help?

Contact Sharm Shutters on WhatsApp or arrange a free site visit to discuss your property.
